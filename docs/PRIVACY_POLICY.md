# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 3, 2026**

Covrd is built with a fundamental principle: **your data is yours.** This policy explains how we achieve that.

## 1. Zero Server-Side Data Handling

Covrd is a 100% client-side application. We do not have servers, databases, or any backend infrastructure that processes or stores your schedules, templates, or employee data. Everything stays on your device.

## 2. No Accounts or Authentication

Because your data never leaves your device, we do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or log in to use Covrd.

## 3. Local Data Storage

All information entered into Covrd is stored entirely locally on your device using standard browser technologies:

- **IndexedDB:** Used for saving employees, schedules, and templates.
- **LocalStorage:** Used for UI preferences (like hiding the onboarding wizard).
- **URL Hash Fragments:** When using the sharing feature, data is compressed and placed into the URL hash fragment (`#`). Hash fragments are never sent to a server.

## 4. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use tracking pixels, analytics scripts (like Google Analytics), or any telemetry to monitor how you use the app.

## 5. No Cookies

Covrd uses zero cookies. Because we do not track you or process data on a server, you won't see a cookie banner here—there is simply nothing to consent to.

## 6. One External Request (Google Fonts)

To ensure accessibility and design consistency, Covrd loads web fonts from Google Fonts CDN. This is the only external network request the app makes. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy regarding data they may temporarily process when fulfilling font requests. We use no other third-party SDKs or scripts.

## 7. Your Control

You have full, absolute control over your data.

- **Export:** You can export your data at any time (JSON, CSV).
- **Delete:** You can delete all your Covrd data instantly by clearing your browser data or using the reset functions within the app.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change the core architecture in a way that affects privacy, we will update this document and the app's version accordingly.

## Contact

Since we don't operate servers or collect data natively, we don't have a direct support channel, but you can report issues or view the source code on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/JoshDoesIT/Covrd).
